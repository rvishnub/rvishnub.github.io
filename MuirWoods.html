Muir Woods is located north of San Francisco, on the other side of the Golden Gate Bridge. It is a beautiful place. A boardwalk was placed so that visitors may walk through the forest without disturbing the trees and plants. The trees are old and so large that a car could pass through the trunk. I felt very small and young -- I had felt the same way looking upon the large foothills of the Himalayas.



If you drive all the way past the Woods, you will eventually arrive at Muir Beach. It took at least an hour to drive from the elevation of the Woods to the beach.



We had a wonderful time climbing the rocks, wading in the ocean, and watching for whales.
