Dharamsala

Dharamsala is the place in India where the Dalai Lama lives in exile.

We flew there from Delhi in a small plane.

Dharamsala is a small town surrounded by mountains.

We visited the Dalai Lama's temple.

We also visited a Tibetan Art Institute.

Our hotel had a small Buddhist shrine.
