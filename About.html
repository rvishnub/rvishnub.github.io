About Me

I was born in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada. I moved to the United States when I was 2 years old. My family lived in New Jersey, then Philadelphia, and then Milwaukee. I grew up in Glendale. I went to the University of Wisconsin-Madison and graduated with a Bachelor of Science with Honors in Molecular Biology.

I eventually got a job with a law firm in Milwaukee as a marketing assistant. This grew into a wonderful career running a nonprofit legal trade association: The National Association of Minority and Women Law Firms.

I attended Marquette University Law School and graduated in 2010. I initially worked for the same law firm, but eventually started my own business.
